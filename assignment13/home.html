Issac is a music producer and artist based in South Chicagoland. His musical journey so far is a testament to his dedication and passion for the art. Blending diverse musical elements, Issac’s productions stand out for their originality and depth, a distinct style that draws inspiration from his surroundings. His commitment to his craft and his relentless pursuit of musical excellence are what set Isaac apart in the landscape of music production.

@1ssaxx archive · nun to me (kenshin mix)

In 2022, Issac released a drum and bass remix of "Nun to Me" by Kankan. The remix featured the typical fast tempo and breaks of the drum and bass genre, while preserving the original melody of the song. This work added a new dimension to Isaac's portfolio and was well-received by fans of both Kankan and the dnb genre. It was a notable project in Issac's career as it gained more attention than any other song at the time.

This track is from early 2022, featuring the first beat ever produced by Issac, a significant step in his musical journey. while its nothing special now, the simplicity of his initial beat can still be appreciated. This song is a important point in Issac's growth as an artist since his first foray into music production.
