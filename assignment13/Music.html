In December 2022, Issac released a drum and bass remix of "Nun to Me" by Kankan. The remix featured the typical fast tempo and breaks of the drum and bass genre, while preserving the original melody of the song. This work added a new dimension to Isaac's portfolio and was well-received by fans of both Kankan and the dnb genre. It was a notable project in Issac's career as it gained more attention than any other song at the time.
