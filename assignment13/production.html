Issac started producing in early 2022, taking inspiration from producers like Pharell, Benjicold, Mally Raw, and more. While he started making beats as a hobby, and didn't take it too seriously, its now one of his favorite things to do and allows him to wrok with several other artists and spread his work further than ever before. This first video is a recent beat made in October 2023. It combines many different sounds from synths to flutes with some hard hitting and unique drums to make a different kind of sound. It features one of the key element in issac's beats, a unique accent bass sound in the bassline. He also likes to use any other kinds of unorthodox sounds for the bass line in songs. The second is a beat made in mid 2022, helping us see the progression over these 2 years of producing. It features some bells and a distinct lead throughout the song. The final track is from early 2022, featuring the first beat ever produced by Issac, a significant step in his musical journey. while its nothing special now, the simplicity of his initial beat can still be appreciated. This song is a important point in Issac's growth as an artist since his first foray into music production.
